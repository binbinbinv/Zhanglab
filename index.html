Peking University Health Science Center

Mingshu Zhang Lab

We develop novel fluorescent probes and apply them to biomedical imaging and detection by integrating protein engineering, artificial intelligence, photophysics, biochemistry, and other interdisciplinary approaches.

Protein Engineering AI for Science Biomedical Imaging

Research Focus

Research

01

Smart Probes for Higher-Resolution Imaging

See more clearly

We study new principles and routes for regulating the performance of optically controlled fluorescent probes and establish broadly applicable strategies for developing super-resolution fluorescent probes.

02

Live-Cell Super-Resolution Imaging Probes

See deeper

We develop high-temporal-resolution super-resolution imaging probes and companion algorithms to precisely localize and trace smaller, faster-moving targets in live cells.

03

Correlative Light-Electron Super-Resolution Imaging Probes

See more faithfully

We develop super-resolution fluorescent imaging probes compatible with both light and electron microscopy to address labeling and precise registration challenges in correlative light-electron imaging.

Team

Mingshu Zhang
Lab Head / Research Professor

Recipient of the National Science Fund for Excellent Young Scholars, the inaugural Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program, the CAS Youth Innovation Promotion Association Excellence Support Program, and the Lu Jiaxi Young Talent Award. Dr. Zhang focuses on deep-tissue, super-resolution, and multimodal bio-optical imaging probes and labeling technologies, fluorescence-imaging-based studies of disease mechanisms, and applications in disease diagnosis, precision stratification, and drug screening.
